Love you from the star

A Dating simulator game Where you will play as Soymilk vendor named Oun destined to meet his love Kao

How to play

Simply run our app in cygwin and play by choosing the way story progress through typing 1 or 2 in the terminal.

Any of the choice you made throughout the game will impact the ending of the story. So choose wisely.

Story

Kao is a librarian who worked nearby a soy milk vendor with Oun being the vendor instead of Oun's dad. With each passing day, their relationship will change either for the better or worse. How will be, depends on you
