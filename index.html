Love you from the star

A Dating simulator game Where you will play as Soymilk vendor named Oun Who about to meet his first love Kao

How to play

First run Our app then simply when to make your choice type in the choice you choose in the terminal

but be careful! your choice have impact on the ending so choose wisely!

Story

One day Kao went to work buy Soymilk as usual but find Oun(The player) as vendor instead of Oun's dad when Oun look at Kao he has feeling for him your adventure to conquer Kao's heart start from now on!
